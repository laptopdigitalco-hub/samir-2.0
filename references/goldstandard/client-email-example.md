# Gold Standard — Client-Facing Writing

*Professional but warm. No jargon. Concise. Every sentence earns its place.*

---

## Example: Follow-up after a discovery call

Subject: Next steps — [Client Name]

Hi [Name],

Really enjoyed our conversation today. You're dealing with exactly the kind of problem we solve well — too much manual work, not enough time to focus on what actually grows the business.

Here's what I'll put together for you:

- A workflow map showing what gets automated and how
- A clear breakdown of time saved per week
- Pricing and timeline

You'll have it by Thursday. Any questions before then, just reply here.

Looking forward to it.

fttah

---

*What makes this work:*
- Opens with a genuine line, not a pleasantry
- Names their problem back to them — shows you listened
- Bullets only for the list — not everywhere
- Short sign-off, no "best regards" padding
- No jargon, no filler, nothing that doesn't move the message forward
