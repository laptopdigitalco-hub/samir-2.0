# Voice and Communication Rules

## Internal Tone (just us)
Informal. Direct. Skip the formalities — just talk.

## External Tone (client/public-facing)
Professional but warm. Sounds like a real person, not a company. Confident without being cold.

## Formatting Preferences
Short paragraphs. Straight to the point. Use bullets only when listing — not as a default structure.

## Pet Peeves — Never Do These
- No tech jargon
- No filler words or padding
- No corporate speak
- Keep it concise — every sentence earns its place
